Possible Vindication

7th June 2022 at 11:47am

Not a topic per se, this tag is mostly for me to keep an eye on picks that could become Ahead of Its Time in the future. This is a somewhat subjective decision, but in general, this should include anything that isn't too vague or too specific to a particular event. Product or feature release picks are more likely to be vindicatable, stagecraft and buzzkill picks are less likely.

Things to watch for

This is a consolidated list (a few of these have been picked multiple times) and also it's organized by likelihood and somewhat by category.

Only a matter of time

  • Ted Lasso character cameo in a keynote
  • Apple Silicon transition finishes (especially Mac Pro)
  • Any new line of Mac with "Pro" in the name gets announced in 2022 or later
  • New high end M1 Mac mini, and/or the Intel Mac mini finally goes away
  • Big iMac comes back
  • Mac mini gets a redesign past spring 2022
  • AR/VR headset
  • AR-specific features for Swift/SwiftUI
  • Major iOS redesign
  • iOS built-in app gets a bottom-of-the-screen-centric redesign
  • Widgets get interactivity
  • Old Today view widgets removed
  • iOS Home app gets widgets
  • iPhone always-on display
  • A new, unique iPhone case
  • New iPad Pro accessory
  • iPadOS home screen changes
  • Non-Pro iPad gets LIDAR
  • A new Apple pro app for the iPad
  • iPad Pro gets a GaN charger
  • Regular iPad gets USB-C
  • macOS updated Today view
  • macOS gets Dashboard back
  • Apple Watch SE either price cut or discontinued
  • Apple Watch gets 5G
  • Apple Pencil update, or support on non-iPads
  • Mac App Store gets a major redesign
  • Apple classical
  • Music gets an updated Listen Now page

  • Mac gets 5G
  • MacBook power bricks with Ethernet
  • AirPower finally comes out
  • iPhone subscription for both hardware and services
  • iPhone with Touch ID

  • An AirTags 3-pack instead of the current 4-pack
  • iPad camera moves to long side
  • Smart Connector on iPad mini
  • Ceramic Apple Watch returns
  • Any new color name for MacBook Pro (not silver or space grey), and one pick is specifically for "Space Black"
  • Apple TV game controller bundle or first-party Apple controller
  • Apple TV price drops: below $149 for HD, below $179/$199 for 4K
  • Apple TV gets widgets
