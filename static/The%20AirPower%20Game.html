Myke wants to know: when is AirPower going to come out? Before the 2018 iPhone event, during, or after? (The idea that it wouldn't come out at all apparently never occurred to him, or anyone else for that matter.) Federico wants to play for prizes, not just bragging rights, so Myke promises to come up with a prize, but that doesn't ever happen.

At the beginning of 2019 in episode 225, Stephen and Myke had finally accepted its fate, but Federico still held out 80% hope that AirPower still would come out eventually. Since this was listed in the document as a 2018 game, Myke declares that the clock has run out with no winner. Apple made it official at the end of March that year.
