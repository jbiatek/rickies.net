The Quizzies

17th August 2022 at 11:52pm

Oh, hello. Thanks for poking around! I take it as a compliment: you care enough to explore past the front door of this silly site I've made. It's a labor of love for The Passionate Ones, after all.

The thing is, yes, I really do want to catalog The Quizzies as well. Very badly. There are a few things preventing me from doing so, though:

  • Some of the quiz questions are members-only content. Sharing that feels like it'd be crossing a line as a fan of the show.
  • Having just the free content would be incomplete, and we are quite thorough around here.
  • Honestly, I'm not sure if it needs the "covered in exhaustive detail" treatment. Maybe I'm not being creative enough, but the "scores and stats" of such a site would just kind of be the current scores and how much each question was worth. I'd be replicating Myke's spreadsheet, really.

But, you know, you came all this way. I don't want to leave you empty handed. So instead of a full blown catalog, please accept this mere index of The Quizzies, formerly known as The Connected Quiz:

  • Relay FM Crossover #187 (available to any Relay FM members)
  • Connected #399, The Rickies
  • Connected #401, The WWDC Keynote (also a Rickies episode)
  • Connected #405, "Do You Know The Passionate Ones", Round 1
  • Connected #406, DYKTPO Round 2, with the debut of the airhorn
  • Connected #409, DYKTPO Round 3 (plus a bit more in the Pro show)
  • Connected #411, What's On Myke's Home Screen (and an actual name for the game)
