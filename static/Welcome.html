This is Rickipedia, a comprehensive repository of information about the ever-evolving Apple prediction game played on Connected by hosts Stephen Hackett, Myke Hurley, and Federico Viticci.

“Better than, maybe, anything else I’ve ever seen on the Internet ever”
— Myke Hurley, Connected #344

Browse the extensive selection of scores and stats, a breakdown of picks by topic, a copy of The Bill of Rickies with a changelog, search the database or just the picks, and more.

✨ What's new

Get excited, it's a new statistic! Some speculation in the latest game about Federico's "longest Risky pick" got me curious, and now picks can be looked at by length. This is the closest I will ever get to the Longest Thigh competition. The API also gained something from this, picks that were too long to fit into the title now have an extra field containing the proper canonical text rather than relying on a convention that, as it turns out, wasn't even as consistent as I thought it was.

It's basically September, which of course means it's time to raise money for St. Jude! The entire Connected Informatic Wikiverse is fundraising again this year for this incredible cause. Last year, the Rickipedia API was a result of reaching milestones, and this year the newest Connected game The Heiries could get the wiki tracker treatment. As a stretch goal, if enough money is raised, I'll be live streaming the styling of the new site — I've got some ideas but the peanut gallery may have some influence on things, so things could get a little silly.
